# Water Injection Optimization Using Historical Production and Seismic Data

利用历史生产和地震资料优化注水

# Abstract

This work proposes a method to optimize waterflooding by using production and time-lapse seismic data. Using timelapse seismic difference as an indication of inter-well influence, inter-well connectivities or influence functions are determined using a combinatorial algorithm (stochastic hillclimbing) by constraining the computation with time-lapse seismic. The water-cut is estimated using a fitting function. By perturbing the injection rate in the injectors, the oil production is optimized. The field in this study is mature with 15 years of production history and with 70% water-cut at the present time. Two legacy repeated seismic surveys, of in 1991 and 2001 vintages respectively, are reprocessed to enhance the repeatability from the original data. The reprocessing is done to eliminate the survey, source and noise differences. The connectivity or influence function is estimated using a window of balanced flooding. It is found that seismic constraining for the connectivity calculation increases the consistency of the inter-well relationship with the spatial data. In order to have an operational waterflooding scheme, a multiple realization approach is used. That is, the optimized injection is randomly perturbed under specified upper and lower bounds. The production rate is computed using the inter-well influence function. It is found that the optimal injection configuration can increase oil production. With a pilot implementation, it is shown that the new injection scheme can delay the oil production decline.

本文提出了一种利用生产数据和时移地震数据优化注水开发的方法。使用时移地震差作为井间影响的指示，通过用时移地震约束计算，使用组合算法（随机爬山）来确定井间连通性或影响函数。利用拟合函数估算含水率。通过扰动注入器中的注入速率，优化石油生产。本次研究的油田已经成熟，有 15 年的开采历史，目前含水率为 70%。对 1991 年和 2001 年的两次重复地震勘探进行了重新处理，以提高原始数据的重复性。进行再处理是为了消除测量、源和噪声差异。使用平衡泛洪的窗口来估计连通性或影响函数。研究发现，连通性计算的地震约束增加了井间关系与空间数据的一致性。为了获得一个可操作的注水方案，使用了一种多重实现方法。也就是说，在指定的上限和下限下随机扰动优化的注入。利用井间影响函数计算产量。结果表明，优化后的注入结构可以提高原油产量。矿场试验结果表明，新的注水方案能够延缓油井产量的递减。

# Introduction

Optimization of water-flooding is a critical process for reservoir management. Generally, the optimization can be conducted through an adjustment of the injection pattern or injection scheduling by reservoir simulation or by intuitive analysis. However, to perform this process, the injector-producer relationship or inter-well relation needs to be understood. To understand this relationship, the production history data are normally used either through a correlation or material balance-type analysis. For example Soeriawinata et al used a correlation approach to build the injector-producer relationship.1 Jansen used a wavelet transform to analyze the inter-well relationship using producer-injector data.2 Albertoni et. al. proposed an approach of linear regression using injector-producer production history data.3,4 For quantitative analysis, reservoir simulation can be used with even seismic data in the process of history matching.5 However, reservoir simulation is quite time-consuming. On the other hand, using the production history sometimes has difficulties to fully account for the spatial heterogeneity. It would be critical to incorporate the production history data and other data such as seismic to analyze the producer-injector relationship. In this work, we propose using the time-lapse seismic attributes to constrain the estimation of the injector-producer relationship.

注水优化是油藏管理的关键环节。通常，可以通过油藏模拟或通过直观分析调整注入模式或注入调度来进行优化。然而，为了执行该过程，需要理解注入井 - 生产井关系或井间关系。为了理解这种关系，生产历史数据通常通过相关性或物料平衡类型分析来使用。例如，Soeriawinata 等人使用相关性方法来建立注入井 - 生产者关系。 Jansen 使用小波变换来使用生产者 - 注入者数据来分析井间关系。Albertoni 等人提出了一种利用注采生产历史数据进行线性回归的方法。为了定量分析，在历史拟合过程中，可以使用均匀的地震数据进行油藏模拟。另一方面，使用生产历史有时有困难充分解释空间异质性。将生产历史数据和其他数据（如地震）结合起来，以分析生产层 - 注入层的关系是至关重要的。在这项工作中，我们建议使用时移地震属性来约束注入 - 生产者关系的估计。

However, the ‘connectivity’ or ‘relationship’ factor is related to the reservoir heterogeneity. Some data such as the time-lapse seismic difference can be a strong indicator for inter-well influence, especially for a waterflooded reservoir. The time-lapse seismic difference is a reflection of the fluid substitution in the process of reservoir production or other dynamic property changes such as pressure or solution gas. This is a strong indication for inter-well influence or ‘connectivity’ (See Figure 1). However, the seismic difference is a semi-quantitative measure of the inter-well relationship. To modify the inter-well relationship, it is necessary to use this information for constraining the determination of the weighting functions.

然而，“连通性” 或 “关系” 因素与储层非均质性有关。诸如时移地震差之类的一些数据可以是井间影响的强指示器，尤其是对于注水储层。时移地震差异是储层开采过程中流体置换或压力、溶解气等其他动态性质变化的反映。这是井间影响或 “连通性” 的强烈迹象（见图 1）。然而，地震差异是井间关系的半定量测量。为了修改井间关系，有必要使用该信息来约束加权函数的确定。

This being the case, these weights may thus be used to determine an optimum schedule of injection/production.

# Field background

实地背景

The field for this study is a reservoir in a faulted structural high with fluvial depositional system. The field starts to produce from 1992. The field has 469 wells in total with 87 injection wells. The field’s production increases rapidly from 1992 to 1995 in the development process, and starts to decline continuously thereafter. Water-cut keeps increasing from 1994. The current water-cut is about 70% (see Figure 2). Even though the water-cut is not very high, the improvement of production seems to be difficult in recent years. The current production status is also shown in Figure 2. Various enhancement processes, including ftacturing, recompletion etc., have been applied in the field to boost the production. Due to the field’s high degree of heterogeneity, it seems that the decline of production is difficult to slow down. The goal of the study is to optimize the water-flooding to improve the production especially using the seismic and production data.

本研究的油田是位于断层构造高地上的一个储油层，具有河流沉积系统。该油田从 1992 年开始生产。油田共有 469 口井，其中 87 口为注水井。该油田的产量在 1992 年至 1995 年的开发过程中迅速增加，之后开始持续下降。从 1994 年开始，断水量不断增加。目前的断水率约为 70%（见图 2）。尽管断水量不是很高，但近年来产量似乎很难提高。生产现状也如图 2 所示。为了提高产量，该油田采用了各种增产工艺，包括压裂、重新完井等。由于该油田的高度异质性，产量的下降似乎难以减缓。本研究的目标是优化注水，特别是利用地震和生产数据来提高产量。

# Time-lapse seismic

时延地震

For the field study, a legacy time-lapse seismic data set is available. The first 3D seismic was acquired in 1991 just before the full field production. The second 3D seismic survey was acquired in 2001. The two surveys have tremendous differences in acquisition parameters. The two surveys were reprocessed simultaneously to eliminate the differences in acquisition direction/azimuth, bin size and folds, sources and noises. It is observed that the reprocessing greatly improves the repeatability. Figure 3 is a comparison of correlation coefficients for each trace and a time window of an inline, which can be a representation of the repeatability for original data after cross-equalization and reprocessed data. From the figure, we can observe the improvement from the reprocessing. Figure 4 is the difference along the target zone. Detailed analysis for the reliability of the difference is performed through a rock physical model and reconciliation with the production data. From the comparison with synthetic difference by simulation model, we can see that there is a similarity between the reprocessed difference and the synthetic. It is concluded that the difference outside the reservoir region is due to the fold difference between the surveys. However, due to the complexity of the difference which is influenced by fluid, pressure and reservoir configuration, it would be difficult to compute the remaining saturation especially for the short inter-well distance reservoir.

在实地考察中，可利用遗留的延时地震数据集。第一次三维地震勘探是在 1991 年油田全面投产前进行的。第二次三维地震勘探是在 2001 年进行的。两次勘探在采集参数上存在巨大差异。对两次勘探同时进行了再处理，以消除采集方向 / 方位角、分区大小和褶皱、震源和噪声等方面的差异。据观察，再处理大大提高了重复性。图 3 是每个迹线和内联时间窗的相关系数比较，可以表示交叉均衡后的原始数据和再处理数据的重复性。从图中我们可以观察到再处理后的改进。图 4 是沿目标区的差值。通过岩石物理模型以及与生产数据的核对，对差值的可靠性进行了详细分析。从与模拟模型合成差值的对比中可以看出，再处理差值与合成差值有相似之处。由此得出结论，储层区域外的差异是由于两次勘测之间的褶皱差异造成的。然而，由于差值受流体、压力和储层构造的影响，非常复杂，因此很难计算剩余饱和度，尤其是对于井间距离较短的储层。

# Inter-well relationship determination

确定井间关系

Using the method described above, the inter-well influence relationship is analyzed. A window of historical data from 1996 to 2001 is used to determine the inter-well relationship. Through analysis of the production data and well spacing, an inter-well influence radius of 900 meters is used. Figure 5 shows the history matching after using the production data only. Using the seismic data as a constraint, the inter-well relationship is computed using the stochastic hillclimbing approach. Figure 6 represents the history matching after using the seismic difference as a constraint. We can see that using the seismic constraint does improve the history matching. In other words, the spatial indication of connectivity is able to improve the reproduction of history data. Also, using the stochastic hillclimbing approach is easy to avoid the negative connectivities which are sometimes difficult to be interpreted. Figure 7 shows the connectivity map using production data only. Figure 8 illustrates the connectivity map using the seismic difference as a constraint. We can see that the connectivity is consistent with difference map. For example, in the marked locations, a high difference corresponds to a strong connectivity. Also, to validate the connectivity, the inter-well relation is analyzed using other information including model, well-to-well log correlation and possible fracture distribution.

利用上述方法，对井间影响关系进行分析。使用 1996 年至 2001 年的历史数据窗口来确定井间关系。通过对生产数据和井间距的分析，确定井间影响半径为 900 米。图 5 显示了仅使用生产数据后的历史匹配情况。以地震数据为约束条件，采用随机爬坡法计算井间关系。图 6 显示了使用地震差值作为约束条件后的历史匹配结果。我们可以看到，使用地震约束确实改善了历史匹配。换句话说，连通性的空间指示能够改善历史数据的再现。此外，使用随机爬山法也很容易避免有时难以解释的负连通性。图 7 显示了仅使用生产数据绘制的连接图。图 8 展示了以地震差异为约束条件的连通性图。我们可以看到，连通性与差值图是一致的。例如，在标注的位置，高差值对应于强连通性。此外，为了验证连通性，还利用其他信息分析了井间关系，包括模型、井间测井曲线相关性和可能的裂缝分布。

Optimization of waterflooding

优化注水

Because the inter-well relationship builds a relationship between the rates of the injectors and producers, given an injection rate at an injector, the production rate can be forecasted for that injection rate. Without changing the well location and well type, if the injection rate can be perturbed to search for an optimal production rate, the waterflood can be optimized. The connectivity determined using the production and seismic data is used for optimization. The first try is to optimize the entire reservoir system. In the process of optimization, the injection rate is constrained by its injection capacity specified by the operator or based on the maximum observed in the history. The yearly total injection rate is set to be the same as that of the previous year. It is found that through optimization it is feasible that the reservoir production rate may be improved 15% compared to the natural decline. Figure 9 shows the optimization result. Figure 10 shows the historical average injection rate distribution. We can see that the injection rate distributes quite uniformly. The underlying map is the time-lapse seismic difference which indicates a strong heterogeneity. After optimization, the injection rate is allocated as in Figure 11. From this new injection reallocation, we can see that the injection follows the time-lapse seismic difference.

由于井间关系在注水井和生产井的注水率之间建立了一种关系，因此给定一个注水井的注水率，就可以预测该注水率的生产率。在不改变油井位置和油井类型的情况下，如果可以通过扰动注入率来寻找最佳生产率，就可以优化注水。利用生产和地震数据确定的连通性可用于优化。首先尝试优化整个储层系统。在优化过程中，注水率受作业者规定的注水能力或历史上观测到的最大注水能力的限制。每年的总注入率设定为与上一年相同。优化结果表明，与自然衰退相比，油藏生产率提高 15% 是可行的。图 9 显示了优化结果。图 10 显示了历史平均注入率分布。我们可以看到，注入率分布相当均匀。底图是延时地震差异图，显示出很强的异质性。优化后，注入率的分配如图 11 所示。从这个新的注入量重新分配图中，我们可以看到注入量是按照延时地震差分布的。

# Pilot implementation

试点实施

Based on the field optimization, the operator chose a pilot area for implementation (see Figure 12). In order to optimize the pilot area, the optimization needs to be performed with a different constraint. The assumption is that the injection outside the pilot area is kept at the same rate of previous year. The only variation in injection is in the pilot study area. Figure 13 is the comparison for the pilot between the pervious year’s injection rate and the optimized rate. Figure 14 is the production status for the pilot area. Figure 15 is the possible oil production improvement in the pilot area. For practical purposes, the designed injection rate needs to be within an operational range. To generate that, an approach of multi-realization is used. That is, a few realizations are generated under the injection uncertainty. The injection rate is computed as Equation 6.

在实地优化的基础上，运营商选择了一个试点区域进行实施（见图 12）。为了优化试点区域，需要在不同的约束条件下进行优化。假定试点区域外的注水量保持与往年相同。注入量的唯一变化是在试点研究区域内。图 13 是试验区上一年注水量与优化注水量的对比。图 14 是试验区的生产状况。图 15 是试验区可能提高的石油产量。出于实际目的，设计的注入率必须在可操作的范围内。为此，我们采用了多重实现的方法。也就是说，在注入量不确定的情况下，会产生几种实际情况。注入率的计算公式为公式 6。

# Results

After implementation, the production is followed up for a few months. Figure 19 is the production curve for the pilot area. We can see that the oil production decline is turned around for 3 months. This indicates that the optimization does help to enhance the production. A detailed single well production analysis is also performed. Figure 20 is a comparison of predicted oil production improvement and the actual oil production improvement for producers in the pilot area. We can see that the improvement is quite consistent. However, we do not expect the oil production to continually increase over time. It will decline at a certain point of time. The optimization should be applied again after certain period of time such that the decline can be arrested or reversed.

实施后，对生产情况进行了几个月的跟踪。图 19 是试点地区的产量曲线。我们可以看到，石油产量下降的趋势在 3 个月内得到扭转。这表明优化确实有助于提高产量。我们还对单井产量进行了详细分析。图 20 比较了试验区生产商的预测石油产量改进和实际石油产量改进。我们可以看到，改进效果相当一致。不过，我们并不期望石油产量随着时间的推移持续增长。它会在某个时间点下降。在一段时间后，应再次进行优化，以阻止或扭转下降趋势。

# Conclusions and discussions

结论和讨论

Based on the field study and pilot implementation, we can see that the waterflooding optimization for mature field is quite feasible and beneficial. We can conclude the following.

根据现场研究和试点实施情况，我们可以看出成熟油田的注水优化是相当可行和有益的。我们可以得出以下结论。

(1) Time-lapse seismic can be used to modify or constrain the determination of connectivity or interwell influence. This constraint highly improves the representation of reservoir heterogeneity and interwell influence.

(2) A non-linear optimization algorithm can be used if the spatial connectivity constraint is used. The stochastic hillclimbing approach is able to solve the constrained linear function efficiently. With this algorithm, an irregular connectivity configuration can be applied. That is, the inter-well connectivity can be edited according to faults and reservoir boundaries. Also, the negative connectivity factors can be avoided.

(3) Based on water-cut prediction, the inter-well relationship can be used for water injection rate optimization. The optimization is based on the well injection capacity and history data. Also, different scenarios can be set for optimization to cater for changes in water supply availability.

(4) Multiple realizations are necessary for an operational injection design. This is useful for understanding the production improvement uncertainties.

(1) 延时地震可用于修改或制约连通性或井间影响的确定，这种约束可大大提高储层异质性和井间影响的代表性。

(2) 如果使用空间连通性约束，可以使用非线性优化算法。随机爬山法能够高效地求解约束线性函数。使用这种算法，可以采用不规则的连通性配置。也就是说，井间连通性可以根据断层和储层边界进行编辑。此外，还可以避免负连通性因素。

(3) 在断水预测的基础上，井间关系可用于注水率优化。优化基于油井注入能力和历史数据。此外，还可以设置不同的优化方案，以应对供水量的变化。

(4) 运行注水设计需要多次实现。这有助于了解提高产量的不确定性。
